Helping Immigrants and Minoritie Succeed.

Our Mission

At Free-Jobs Inc, we are on a mission to help immigrants and minorities (BIPOC) succeed by providing them with the necessary tools, resources, and guidance to integrate seamlessly into the USA. We believe that everyone deserves a fair chance to thrive and achieve their full potential in their adopted home, regardless of their background or circumstance.

Our Vision

Be among the top 10 resources hubs for immigrants and minorities.

Our comprehensive programs and valuable educational resources are designed to help you overcome the challenges that come with adapting to a new culture and environment. We collaborate with organizations and individuals to build a strong support network for you in the USA, creating an inclusive environment that embraces diversity and fosters a sense of belonging for all.

From humble beginnings to Free-Jobs ...

My journey began in Accra, Ghana from humble beginnings but it didn’t end there. Despite my big dreams, it was when I got to America that I saw the opportunity to live my dreams. Yes,it hasn’t been smooth sailing, but I am learned so much and I can certainly say that it is possible to live your dreams. I am a true testament of a Free-Jobs in the making. Our Values

We empower immigrants and minorities (BIPOC) to succeed in the USA.

Through our honest, transparent, and ethical approach, we are accountable to the communities we serve, our donors, and partners. We respect everyone’s potential and treat them with equality and dignity, assuming they have value and integrity. Our leadership enables you to make the most of the opportunities available, and to contribute to their communities both locally and globally.

At Free-Jobs Inc, we are dedicated to guiding immigrants to achieve career success, as we believe that this is an essential part of integration. Through our resources that help immigrants integrate, we provide expert advice and a supportive community that helps newcomers like you build the skills and confidence needed to pursue their career goals and excel in their chosen field.

Integrity

We uphold ethical values & conduct business with transparency and honesty. We are accountable to the communities we serve, our donors & partners, while helping immigrants and minorities succeed.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all our endeavors and work tirelessly to achieve our mission, providing high-quality programs and resources that meet the unique needs of our clients.

Respect

We value diversity and inclusivity, treating everyone with respect and dignity, and recognizing their unique potential to contribute to society.

Leadership

We lead by example, empowering individuals and communities to achieve their goals and thrive, while promoting positive change and social justice.

Our Team...

Our Board Members

Our Board members come from a diverse professional and philanthropic backgrounds. They are committed to supporting Free-Jobs and its strategic initiatives and leveraging corporate, non-profit and philanthropic relationships to ensure sufficient resources is available to advance our impact, as we strive towards helping immigrants and minorities succeed through our programs in the communities we serve.

Board members share the belief that every person is endowed with potential and deserves to live life to the fullest as a Free-Jobs. They believe in the strength and tremendous abilities of our immigrant and minority communities, the hard work and diverse perspectives that we all bring to the table to make America great, and a conviction that this same global diversity will help to solve the challenges that lay before us. Our vision is of a United States where immigrants and minorities can seamlessly integrate into the professional workforce and the fabric of American life and be recognized for the value they bring.

Our Commitment...

DIVERSITY, EQUITY, INCLUSION AND ACCESSIBILITY (DEIA)

Master-Pieced Inc is committed to fostering a community that embraces diversity, equity, inclusion and accessibility through the programs we offer. We affirm one another’s differences by honoring the many cultures, perspectives and experiences that we bring – through our programs and engagement with the communities we serve, and our efforts to create and amplify a culture based on acceptance and inclusivity, we believe these values will lead us toward a more equitable society.

Our Future

We recognize that our DEIA efforts will be ongoing and evolving, and we commit to making it a priority to regularly evaluate our organizational practices, programming, communications, hiring practices and accessibility of our facilities through the lens of our DEIA values. Now and in our future, we will continually work to create an inclusive and inspiring experience where all are welcome and feel valued.
